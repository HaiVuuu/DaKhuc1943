# Nocturne 1943: The Torch of Vong Village

**Nocturne 1943** is a Visual Novel set in Vietnam during the historical context of 1943 made for our HCM202 subject's project build in 1 week. The game puts players in the shoes of a young intellectual on a journey to awaken the people's spirit amidst the darkness of colonial rule, confronting superstitions, social evils, and colonial exploitation.

## ðŸ“– Storyline
Players take on the role of **Minh**, an intellectual returning to Vong Village after years away. There, he witnesses the desolation of his homeland under the Japanese-French occupation. Minh must face grueling challenges:
- **Act 1: The Stomach and The Mind** - Persuade Uncle Tu not to demolish the village communal house for timber, protecting the nation's spiritual values.
- **Act 2: Miracle Cure or Poison?** - Uncover the truth about the "holy water" and opium used by Fortune Teller Ba to poison the bodies and wills of the villagers.
- **Act 3: Light and Darkness** - Confront Master Long and his "fake civilization" schemes designed to trick compatriots into forced labor.

## ðŸŽ® Key Features
- **Debate System:** Use collected evidence to refute opponents during high-stakes confrontations.
- **Trust Score:** Every choice and piece of evidence you present affects the villagers' level of trust in you.
- **Poetry Recitation System:** Integrated Han-Nom poems (by Phan Boi Chau, ancient verses...) with translations, creating a nostalgic cultural atmosphere.
- **Exploration & Collection:** Search for clues at locations like the Communal House, Village Gate, and Shrine to unlock critical dialogue options.
- **Multiple Endings:** The fate of Vong Village is in your hands.
